The construction of the Great Pyramid of Giza is one of the greatest engineering feats in history. It is believed to have taken over 20 years to complete and required the labor of thousands of skilled workers. The pyramid stood at an incredible 481 feet tall and was built as a tomb for the pharaoh Khufu. Its construction was a testament to the ingenuity and skill of the ancient Egyptians and it remains one of the most impressive architectural achievements of all time.
